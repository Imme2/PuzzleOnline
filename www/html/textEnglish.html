The Petroglyphs are engraved figures in bas-relief on the surface of some rocks, made with different techniques of direct or indirect percussion, abrasion and/or scratched. Their presence implies, as implied in the past, territoriality. The signs are recognized by members of the same ethnic community, and identified as territorial markers by members of other communities, who fear and respect them.

The Rock Paintings or Pictographs, are drawings made in the rocks through the application of pigments. It is used in some cases minerals as iron oxides, substances of animal origin, such as blood and fat, also coal and clay. Appear in caves and shelters, protected from the weather and not readily visibility.

The Whetstones or Grindstones are oval shaped depressions made by abrasion possibly from sharpening stone implements. Accompanying the Petroglyphs that border the rivers, or in nearby rocks, there are many stones with grooves with forms of Whetstones , which could indicate the shapes of the lithic tools, used to record them.

Interpretations and meanings of the Natural Mythical Hills, in our country, are correlated with the varied and extensive mythologies that still today forms part of the live culture of 30 linguistically differentiated ethnic groups. The Cerro Autana, whose true name is Kuwai (son of Napiruli) , is considered to be the cosmic tree, the tree of life (the yucca of manioc plant) , the centre of the world, connecting the sky , the earth and hell. On this tree were to be found all the seeds of all the fruits which would provide man with sustenance.

The Micropetroglyps are small pebbles or stone slabs with engravings. Its use is unknown. Its restricted distribution has no explanation. They could have been used as mnemonics teaching resources of secrecy, talismans of a specific cultural group, such as divinatory shamanic elements; existing today in the use of snails, or pebbles in santeria, or as divinatory spells.

The Menhirs are Megalithic Monuments formed by large elongated stones, placed in vertical row, with their bottom buried in the ground to stabilize them. Some have engravings on their surface. The existence of engravings on some rocks of the ranks of, Menhirs allows to relate two manifestations -Petroglyphs and Menhirs- like belonging to a same time and community.
